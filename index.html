Paneer Butter Masala

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Tomato
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 Steps:

  1. Heat butter in pan
  2. Add tomato paste & spices
  3. Add paneer cubes
  4. Cook for 10 minutes
  5. Add cream and serve
